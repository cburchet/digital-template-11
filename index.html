Circus Shooter

  • High Concept: An arcade style shooter to score points.
  • Player Experience Goals: Relive playing games at the fair by shooting targets as they scross by.
  • Genre: arcade shooter
  • Game play: Aim with the mouse and left click to shoot. Hit the targets for varying amounts of points and get as many as you can before time runs out.
  • Prototype goal: Creating a "first person shooter" with targets that scroll by and can be shot for points.
  • Features: shooting targets for points, crosshair to show where you are shooting that lights up when over a target.
  • Setting/Premise: abstract
  • Story: na
  • Target Audience: carnival lovers
  • Play time: 2 minutes
  • Strategies: aim for the farther away targets since they are worth far more points and aim slightly ahead of the targets.
